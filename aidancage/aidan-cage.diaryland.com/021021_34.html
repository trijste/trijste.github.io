To Aid An_ Cage

2002-10-21 - 4:30 p.m.

tossed(time)
the stream that spans the girth of time runs thickly through these weeks of guns though bullets they may not fire they fire none the less and the shooting I will do begins to set my gut alight with fright and fear all new to me though not new for I have known the searing belch of deadlines yet now is the time or here it comes it seems better not to watch it as it tumbles through the rocks and eddies to scratch my placid lakes away to be replaced with a ferocious moment already forgotten in the rememberance of it's need to pass these lines of glass too hot to keep they ripple away towards a new day and one less stressed and less likely to crack the time comes for me to toughen my skin and dive deep into the rivers that surround me and flow towards my death all in due time.

PEACE - Tristan
