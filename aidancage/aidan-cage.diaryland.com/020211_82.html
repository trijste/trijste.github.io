To Aid An_ Cage

2002-02-11 - 9:27 a.m.

nothing(owned)
it's exciting to realise that I don't actually own anything. I'm just unfortunate enough to believe I do.

what comes in the way of words? what comes in the way of thoughts, and what comes in the way of thinking? these are three different ways to come across ideas as the first is being spoken to of them, the second would be reading of them, the third actually thinking them. the collective unconscious has been approved for marketing and here we are sitting in a pile of its refuse all the while humming lost songs from the titanic.

it really is the beginning of the day. my head is just marathoning my mind.

PEACE - Tristan
