To Aid An_ Cage

2004-09-07 - 4:37 p.m.

sunsetter
summer suns setting.

floated out to the farm for a week and a half. Really good to spend time with my grandparents and helping my aging grandfather out with different chores he wanted doing. I worked an overnight shoot as a PA for this Nike spot, not exactly my ethical choice, but I didn't know until I had already agreed to it. Good connections out of it. No pay, but I need the experience.

The farm was great though. A freshwater pond to swim in, trails to walk though the trees and the Bruce trail goes along the back fence, so I hiked one afternoon with my mom. I worked on a couple jigsaw puzzles with my grandfather and practised my sax for them. It sucks to come back to the city, but I need to get back in gear to move past this year. What a mess! I don't think I could have had a worse year, or don't want to know how it could get worse. I started the year at the coatcheck room, and now I'm on meds to control my self. I want to go back in time, but I con only move forward. Next year will be better, already a trip planned for going west. There was a trip planned for this year, but I had other things in mind when summer came.

I need to find out if I'm legally obligated to go to the clarke, because if I'm not I will stop going at some point. They don't seem to want to do anything but keep me drugged, and that is a problem for me. I'll have to try to get them to lower my dosage again, and if they don't I'll have to start cutting my pills soon.

I was supposed to start a class at Seneca tonight at seven, but it got pushed back a week. Thursday I start a class on interpersonal communication which I need to work on because I am too quite for my own liking. It isn't that I'm shy, I just often feel I have nothing worth sharing.

Tania is coming back into town tomorrow for a short while. It will be good to see her again.

I miss everyone. I am socially isoplated here in the burbs. I might see Ben tonight. I just want to get out of my house more often now.

Liz is coming back to the city and I would like to spend more time with her this year. She is a good person.

Jenn's still on the island and I'll probably talk to her soon. I missed not seeing her this summer...this summer...what an enchanting time for all.

PEACE - Tristan
