To Aid An_ Cage

2003-06-03 - 11:27 a.m.

passed_time(summary_of)
Why havn't I been updating? There really isn't a reason that is a solid thought I can transfer into solid words and explain in a way in which my thoughts become your thoughts on the same subject. I don't know why specifically. I have been working, though not washing dishes anymore. I am now doing coffee bar and lunch service at a bakery around yonge and eglinton. I am starting to tire of it already. The work itself is not hard, and if I only had to come in and do the work--like it was when I was dish washing, it wouldn't be so bad. But it is more than the work now. I have to worry about the business too--and fuck business. They want me to close the store alone and do all the stupid money math and I just can't be bothered to really care wether the numbers work or to even learn how they should work. They are meaningless symbols to me. They are part of a language that begins to describe a realm of reality that I do not care to exist on. I'm going to quit and hitchhike east instead of west. People I know are heading that way. I know people that are heading west too, but I'd rather head east. I can hit the protests along the way and try to find a piece of myself that my thoughts are unable to touch and feel and develop ideas about.

My trip to Israel fell through. There weren't enough seats booked on the flight, so I got bumped off the super cool adventure trip I wanted to scam. They offered me some other weaker community based trip that left later on in the month of June, but I said no. If I'm scamming a free trip to Israel, I want the best damn trip I can get--even if I have to wait a whole year for it.

I smoke weed. I smoke weed every day. I don't know what else to say about it other than just to document it for myself and others. It is quite a habit I have picked up and the place it takes me when I smoke it is not so interesting anymore. I am know just keeping a habit. I don't feel good or bad, but I continue to smoke. I have taken to climbing things when I smoke weed, so I can get high physically as well as mentally. This keeps it fun and lets me take myself to the solitude that I feel in my head. When I walk around and see all the people on the street, I get this funny feeling of disbelief, like I just don't believe they all exist. Sometimes it is just that I don't care. When I get high--like climbing onto a roof or into a tree, they all disappear and I am reminded of my mental landscape. Does the pot make me feel that way, or do I just feel that way? In anycase I am feeling something like bad these days. Hopefully I'll find something tangible that I can hold onto and have lift me for longer than the short spurts of happiness I get from feeling the sun on my skin.

Today I am off to Shout to work on this video project. I don't really want to work on it anymore because I know it's going to suck and that I wont care. I have some ideas for a script that are starting to work towards coming together. I'd like more time to ground them.

My social life is in the same boat as my treeclimbing and roof jumping rituals. I am rarely around anyone I have true feelings of love for. I am either at work where I interact in a jaded happy way with everyone--customers and staff, or I'm at Shout doing meaningless interviews and film work, or I'm at home where I feel like the bonds with my family that should be so much stronger or falling apart. Around this time of year I start to miss the cottage and my father and seeing his side of the family. It is these times where I feel the fountain of emotion asking me if I want to put on a water show.

The phone just rang and it was Liam and we talked for a bit. It was a great conversation. I feel better and will write later, but now I am late.

PEACE - Tristan
