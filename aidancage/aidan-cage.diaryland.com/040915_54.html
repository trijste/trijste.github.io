2004-09-15 - 10:26 a.m.

wednesday morning smoothie
Mystic Journeys class yesterday. Seems like it will be interesting, but not quite what I had in mind. It is more about the new paradigm of thought that deals with auras and energy and homeopathy. The teachers a little cagey, but it is deffinately something to do and worth doing.

I'm working the Phil Collins concert on Monday, so I can make some coin. Dale called my house last night while I was at class, and I called hime back. He still needs to confirm.

I had a great film idea last night before I went to sleep, but I didn't write it down and I can't remember if it was any good. All I have are some juxtaposed pictures that don't make any sense.

I've got some errands to run today. I have to go get a money order for $10US, so I can send out another copy of my film to a festival. I've got three lined up to send out to three different festivals, so maybe I'll hear back. I've also got to get some photos photocopied. One for my grandfathers B-day, and two others to include as a press package with my films. I've also got to clean the fish box, take a bath, shave, read, smoke, and wait for my mom to get home to end my lonliness and isolated feelings I get from my suburban habitat. I have a lot of social desires, but lack the phoneing friends.

I bought another pack of butts yesterday before a really good sax lesson. It just reaffirmed how much I dislike smoking and want to quit. I will soon.

I'm going up to the tree farm for the weekend, then I'm back down for a possible meeting Sunday night for the short I'm acting in. I'm getting busier, which takes my mind off of the strange visions I had earlier in the year, but I'm still a bit lost and still a bit weakened by them. I crave the easy days of before, where I would just do my thing. Now I think too much about things and don't see reality the way I used to. And I don't like what you've got me hanging from...
