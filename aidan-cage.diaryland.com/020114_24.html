To Aid An_ Cage

2002-01-14 - 9:23 a.m.

second(term)
Today I begin the second term of my five term course in digital television production. I begin under different circumstances than I did the term before. I am no longer a smoker, as I was before. I no longer eat meat, and am quietly working towards a healthy vegan lifestyle. I no longer have the desire to view hardcore pornography, as it has begun to really bother me. I also have the knowledge that I have begun to walk a serious and positive path. I have started going back to the gym, not because I want to become big and strong, but because I enjoy knowing where my body is. I have started to understand and appreciate the opposite sex, though I still have no desire to begin a relationship. I have finally realised that all I can be is myself, and myself is all I want to be. With this in mind, I have finally realised that whatever path I choose to walk will bring me peace, power, passion, and positivity, as long as I choose it and I walk it.

PEACE - Tristan
