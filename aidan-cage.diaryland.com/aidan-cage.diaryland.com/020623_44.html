To Aid An_ Cage

2002-06-23 - 5:23 p.m.

flying(right)
I slept among a thousand dreams singing late in the day to the mantra that is life and all the beautiful drops of light running down from the sky to the eye and finding only love to grace and be graced with quietly within the rising song and only peace in the mind only peace in the winding ways of our lives my fear only fear and my tears only drops of emotion found beautiful in the art of it all and all the while stealing moments from the tree of life and throwing rocks at good and evil and laughing at the convenience of fear and laziness and the passive agressive depression of then and tomorrow and the infinite clarity of the wonder of now all meshing together in the soup of experience sometimes lost amongst the carrots and the peas but always swirling it all together in togetherness like water in my blood and earth in my mind.

PEACE - Tristan
