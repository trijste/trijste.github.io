To Aid An_ Cage

2004-05-04 - 4:37 p.m.

not
watching you watching

your hair bends to curve you and caress your lack of sleep

I see you only when my eyes land closed from flight and leaves gather

I awake to your dreams of me and find a lack of myself

My mind is quiet and suicidal now

I dream of laying down for subways with my head breaking over the yellow line and painting you with my only colour

I hate to eat as it pulls me further from your embrace

I hate to sleep because I know it is when you rise

I fall from you and am ever falling towards you

I have no peace

only pieces

and never enough to finish your puzzle

you trickster

get out of me and see me

not

LOVE - Tristan
