To Aid An_ Cage

2002-03-24 - 12:00 p.m.

less(home)
I also passed a homeless man, and stopped to give him my last bit of money, a nickle in my wallet. He asked me how my day was, and I told him it was pretty good. I asked him how his was, and he said it was incredible. "I woke up this morning." That's all the purity I want. I shook his hand, and tried to take him with me as I walked, but he was already there. He was no longer homeless.

PEACE - Tristan
