To Aid An_ Cage

2003-07-12 - 12:40 p.m.

breakfast
I woke up in Montreal this morning, and in Derek's pad. Paul and I made it to Montreal in about six or so hours, which is pretty good, as hitching goes. Today we stay and party in the city, then split tomorrow for the next leg of our journey. Gross Morne is not too far away, and it is our destination. We move through Quebec and take the ferry to the island paradise that holds the archetype of our holy grail on this journey. See you there.

PEACE - Tristan
