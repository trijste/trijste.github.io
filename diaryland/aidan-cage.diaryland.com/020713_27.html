To Aid An_ Cage

2002-07-13 - 2:56 p.m.

carter's_cottage(burnt)
burnt and burning still under the flesh my skin is redder than my Friday night eyes burning in the sunshine of overcast understanding and feeling pain too present to refund.

burning in the sunlight sunny sky so bright but hiding the gross miscalculation of our comfort covered body and feet in the sun.

burnt for good reason and burning still to know what little there is sleeping under the bug net only creased in the sand like love underfoot.

barefoot burning in fitted booty on ski sliding unnaturaly on the water feeling funny but having fun none-the-less.

burnt out under burning stars in sky wondering why my eyes are closed to it all.

arriving burned in the summer sun returned to house but what of home?

lost in the burning sun some summer steam eased out with a shriek from my boiling kettle burnt the water too bitter sweet to drink.

the smell of something burning coming from the north and descending upon us like rays in the burning sky.

I burn.

PEACE - Tristan
