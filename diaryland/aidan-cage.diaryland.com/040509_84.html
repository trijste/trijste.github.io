feariday
Friday I went out to the zoo with Ashley and Jamieson and Mark and Laura. I havn't been for too long and so was really digging the wandering from habitat to habitat. Ashley is so great. It is hard to think of a life without her help. Her relationship with Jamieson is strange to me. I see how she loves and hates it and I am at a loss to help her into heaven, when she seems to dig her hell--whatever that means. After the zoo, she cooked for Jamieson and I and we ate some great, spicy pasta on her back deck. Then to the basement where we awaited and arrived to Andrews coming--stranger that he is, it was good to see him. Then a game of chess I could call it. I slipped up on my narcotic slide and hit the rocks. Andrew shared his cognac. But I got home safe with a ride from my mom--bless her. Saturday I shared the day with mom, then headed out for a night with Paul and Jonce. Just chilling--we watched the 'Junky's Christmas' by Bill S. I headed home early to catch some Z's before the brunch I shared with my GPrents and Aunt Janice. Wow good food buffet. I'm home now and about to practice the sax.
